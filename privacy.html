Curl it! Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Curl it! ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection in relation to our application, Curl it! (the "App").

By using Curl it!, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We use your data only as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information Collection and Use

At present, Curl it! does not collect any personal information from its users. We have designed our App with a strong emphasis on user privacy and data minimization. Our current practices include:

  • No collection of personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • No collection of device information or IP addresses
  • No user accounts or registration required

We believe that the best way to protect your data is not to collect it in the first place. This approach allows you to use our App with confidence, knowing that your personal information remains private.

3. Local Storage and Data Retention

Curl it! uses local storage on macOS SwiftData for persistent storage. This means:

  • Any data saved is stored only on your device
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • You have full control over the data stored by the App

The data stored locally may include your preferences and settings within the App. This data is used solely to improve your user experience and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We firmly believe in your right to privacy. As such:

  • We do not share any user data with third parties
  • We do not sell, rent, or lease any personal information
  • We do not disclose any information for marketing or advertising purposes

In the unlikely event that we are required by law to disclose any information, we will do so in accordance with applicable legal requirements and will endeavor to limit the scope of any such disclosure.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Curl it! does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies. We do not:

  • Use browser cookies or similar technologies
  • Employ any user tracking or analytics tools
  • Allow third-party tracking on our App

This ensures that your usage of our App remains private and is not tracked or monitored.

6. Data Security

While we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, we still take the security of your locally stored data seriously. We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and data stored locally on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Curl it! is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 13, please contact us at the email address provided below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Your Rights and Choices

As we do not collect personal data, many traditional data protection rights do not apply to our App. However, you still have the right to:

  • Access and control the data stored locally on your device
  • Delete all data associated with the App by uninstalling it from your device
  • Opt out of future communications from us

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Us page for information on how to reach us.

We will make every effort to respond to your inquiries and address your concerns in a timely and effective manner.
